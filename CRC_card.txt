GameFrame:
	1. Responsible for displaying the Game current status and map onto the user's
screen.

Game:
	1. The engine of the game. 
	2. Responsible for listening to the user's input and choose the right
reaction to handle it. 
	3. Responsible for storing and loading the game status and map.
	

GameInfo:
	1. Responsible for storing the Level number, Gameover status

GameMap:
	1. Responsible for storing the map of the game
	2. Responsible for keeping updating the map.

Hero:
	1. Responbile for storing the Hero position, alive status 
	2. Responsible for moving the hero.
